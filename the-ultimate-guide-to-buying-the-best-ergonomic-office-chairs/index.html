Buyer's Guide: 3 Key Factors to Consider

1. Adjustability

To ensure maximum support, look for chairs with adjustable seat height, backrest tilt, and armrests. This allows you to customize the chair to your body's unique needs and maintain a neutral posture.

2. Lumbar Support

Proper lumbar support is crucial for reducing lower back strain. Opt for chairs with adjustable lumbar support to maintain the natural curve of your spine and prevent discomfort.

3. Material and Build Quality

High-quality materials like breathable mesh or durable leather not only enhance comfort but also extend the life of the chair. A well-built chair offers better value and longevity.
