FAQs

1. Are robotic vacuum cleaners worth it?

Yes, robotic vacuum cleaners are worth it for their convenience and efficiency in maintaining clean floors with minimal human intervention.

2. How often should I run my robotic vacuum?

For optimal cleanliness, it's recommended to run your robotic vacuum 3-4 times a week, or daily if you have pets or a high-traffic household.

3. Can robotic vacuums clean all floor types?

Most robotic vacuums are designed to handle a variety of floor types, including hardwood, tile, and low-pile carpets. However, performance may vary on high-pile carpets.

4. How do robotic vacuums navigate?

Robotic vacuums use various navigation technologies such as laser mapping, cameras, and infrared sensors to map and navigate your home efficiently.

5. What maintenance do robotic vacuums require?

Regular maintenance includes emptying the dustbin, cleaning the brushes, and checking for clogs. Some models also require occasional filter changes and battery replacements.
