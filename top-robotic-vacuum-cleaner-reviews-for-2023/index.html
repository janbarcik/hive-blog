FAQ Section

1. How often should I run my robotic vacuum?

Most homes benefit from daily cleaning, but it depends on your lifestyle. Homes with pets or high foot traffic may require more frequent cleaning.

2. Can robotic vacuums replace traditional vacuums?

While robotic vacuums are excellent for maintenance cleaning, they may not completely replace the deep cleaning capabilities of traditional vacuums.

3. How do robotic vacuums navigate around the house?

Most modern robotic vacuums use technologies like LiDAR, cameras, or infrared sensors to map and navigate your home efficiently.

4. Are robotic vacuums suitable for all floor types?

Yes, most robotic vacuums can handle a variety of floor types, including hardwood, tile, and low-pile carpet. However, performance may vary on high-pile carpets.

5. What maintenance do robotic vacuums require?

Regularly empty the dustbin, clean the brushes, and check for any blockages. Some models with self-emptying bases require less frequent maintenance.
