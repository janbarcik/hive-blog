Buyer's Guide: Key Factors to Consider

1. Size & Portability

Measure your available space and compare it with the dimensions of potential machines. Look for lightweight models if you plan to move the machine frequently.

2. Brewing Quality

Consider the machine's pressure level—15 bars is ideal for a rich espresso. Check for additional features like a built-in grinder or milk frother if they are important to you.

3. Ease of Use & Maintenance

Choose a machine with user-friendly controls. Look for detachable components that are dishwasher safe for easier cleaning.
