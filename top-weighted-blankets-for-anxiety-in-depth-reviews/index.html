FAQ Section

1. What weight of a blanket should I choose for anxiety relief?

The general rule of thumb is to choose a blanket that is about 10% of your body weight. However, personal preference and comfort should also be considered.

2. Are weighted blankets safe to use every night?

Yes, weighted blankets are generally safe for nightly use. However, they should not be used by individuals with certain medical conditions or by infants.

3. Can weighted blankets help with other conditions besides anxiety?

Yes, weighted blankets can also help with conditions such as insomnia, ADHD, and autism by promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality.

4. How do I clean a weighted blanket?

Most weighted blankets come with specific care instructions. Many have removable covers that can be machine washed, while the inner blanket may require spot cleaning or dry cleaning.

5. Do weighted blankets work for everyone?

While many people find weighted blankets helpful for anxiety and sleep issues, they may not work for everyone. It's important to try one and see if it meets your needs and comfort levels.
