Buyer's Guide: 3 Key Factors to Consider

1. Sound Quality

When looking for the best wireless earbuds under $100, sound quality is paramount. Consider earbuds that offer customizable sound profiles or EQ settings, allowing you to adjust bass, treble, and mids to your preference. Brands like Anker and JLab offer these features.

2. Battery Life

Battery life is crucial, especially for those who use their earbuds throughout the day. Look for earbuds that offer at least 20 hours of total playtime with the charging case. Models like the Tribit FlyBuds 3 excel in this area with up to 100 hours of playtime.

3. Comfort and Fit

Comfort is key when choosing earbuds, especially for extended use. Look for options with multiple ear tip sizes and ergonomic designs that fit securely in your ears. Brands like Aukey and Tribit often provide a range of sizes for a customized fit.
