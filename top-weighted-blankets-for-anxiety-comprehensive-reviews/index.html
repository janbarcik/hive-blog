FAQ Section

How do weighted blankets help with anxiety?

Weighted blankets provide deep pressure stimulation, which can promote relaxation and reduce anxiety by triggering the release of serotonin and melatonin, while decreasing cortisol levels.

What weight should I choose for my weighted blanket?

It's generally recommended to choose a blanket that is about 10% of your body weight. However, personal preference and comfort are key factors, so consider trying different weights if possible.

Can children use weighted blankets for anxiety?

Yes, but it's crucial to ensure the blanket is appropriate for the child's weight and age. It's best to consult with a pediatrician before using a weighted blanket for a child.

Are there any side effects of using a weighted blanket?

While generally safe, some people may find them too heavy or warm. It's important to ensure the user can move the blanket independently and is comfortable with the weight.

How should I care for my weighted blanket?

Most weighted blankets come with specific care instructions. Generally, it’s advisable to use a removable cover for easy cleaning. Some blankets are machine washable, but always check the manufacturer's guidelines.
