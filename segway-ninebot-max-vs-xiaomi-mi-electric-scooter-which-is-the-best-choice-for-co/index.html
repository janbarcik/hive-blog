Battery Life

When it comes to electric scooters, battery life is crucial for determining how far you can travel on a single charge. The Segway Ninebot Max boasts an impressive range of up to 40.4 miles, making it ideal for longer commutes. In contrast, the Xiaomi Mi Electric Scooter offers a range of about 18.6 miles. While this is sufficient for shorter urban commutes, it might not be enough for longer journeys without a mid-day recharge.

Portability

Portability is another essential factor for commuters who frequently use public transportation or need to carry their scooters up and down stairs. The Xiaomi Mi Electric Scooter shines in this category, weighing approximately 26.9 lbs, making it easier to carry around. The Segway Ninebot Max, while still foldable, is heavier at 41.2 lbs, which might be cumbersome for some users.

Speed

Speed is a key consideration for those looking to reduce their commute time. Both scooters offer a top speed of approximately 15.5 mph, which is standard for electric scooters in this category. Thus, speed might not be the deciding factor for most buyers.

FAQ Section

Is Ninebot made by Xiaomi?

No, Ninebot is a separate company that acquired Segway in 2015. While Xiaomi is a major investor in Ninebot, the two are distinct entities.

Is Segway owned by Xiaomi?

Segway is owned by Ninebot, a Chinese company that counts Xiaomi as one of its investors. However, Xiaomi does not own Segway directly.

Which scooter is more suitable for long distances?

The Segway Ninebot Max is more suitable for long distances due to its longer battery range of up to 40.4 miles.

Which scooter is easier to carry?

The Xiaomi Mi Electric Scooter is easier to carry due to its lighter weight of approximately 26.9 lbs.

Are both scooters foldable?

Yes, both the Segway Ninebot Max and the Xiaomi Mi Electric Scooter feature a foldable design for easier storage and transport.
