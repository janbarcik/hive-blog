FAQ Section

1. Are robotic vacuum cleaners suitable for all floor types?

Yes, most robotic vacuums are designed to handle various floor types, including hardwood, tile, and carpet.

2. How often should I run my robotic vacuum cleaner?

For optimal cleanliness, it's recommended to run your robotic vacuum cleaner daily or at least several times a week, depending on foot traffic and pet presence.

3. Can robotic vacuums clean stairs?

No, robotic vacuums cannot clean stairs. They are designed for flat surfaces, but some models have sensors to prevent falls.

4. How do robotic vacuums handle pet hair?

Many robotic vacuums come equipped with strong suction and brushes designed to pick up pet hair effectively. Models like the Roborock S7 MaxV Ultra are particularly good for pet owners.

5. Do robotic vacuums require Wi-Fi?

While Wi-Fi is not necessary for basic operation, it allows for enhanced features such as app control, scheduling, and smart home integration.
