FAQ Section

What should I consider when choosing a home security camera?

When selecting a home security camera, consider factors such as video resolution, field of view, night vision capabilities, smart home integration, ease of installation, and whether it requires a subscription for cloud storage.

Are wireless security cameras reliable?

Yes, wireless security cameras are reliable and offer flexibility in installation. However, ensure they have a stable Wi-Fi connection and consider battery life if they are not hardwired.

Do home security cameras work without Wi-Fi?

Some home security cameras can work without Wi-Fi, but their functionality may be limited. It's important to check the specifications of each camera to understand its capabilities.
