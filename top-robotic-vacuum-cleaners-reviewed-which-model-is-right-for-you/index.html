FAQ Section

1. What features should I look for in a robotic vacuum cleaner?

When choosing a robotic vacuum, consider features like mapping technology, suction power, battery life, obstacle avoidance, and smart home compatibility.

2. Are robotic vacuums effective on all types of flooring?

Most robotic vacuums are designed to work on a variety of surfaces, including hardwood, tile, and low-pile carpet. However, performance can vary, so it's important to check the specifications for each model.

3. How often should I run my robotic vacuum?

For optimal cleanliness, it's recommended to run your robotic vacuum at least a few times a week. Some models can be scheduled to clean daily.

4. Can robotic vacuums handle pet hair?

Yes, many robotic vacuums are equipped with features specifically designed to handle pet hair, such as strong suction and self-cleaning brushrolls.

5. Do robotic vacuums require much maintenance?

Robotic vacuums generally require minimal maintenance, but it's important to regularly empty the dustbin, clean the filters, and check for tangled hair or debris in the brushes.
