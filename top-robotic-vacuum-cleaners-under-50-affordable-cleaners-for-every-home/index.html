Are the cheap robot vacuums any good?

Yes, cheap robotic vacuums can be quite effective for basic cleaning tasks. While they may not have all the bells and whistles of high-end models, many budget-friendly options still offer good suction power, reliable navigation, and convenient features like auto-docking.

What is the most reliable robotic vacuum?

How long do budget robotic vacuums last?

The lifespan of a budget robotic vacuum can vary, but with proper maintenance, they typically last between 1 to 3 years. Regular cleaning and battery care can extend their longevity.

Can robotic vacuums clean carpets effectively?

Most budget robotic vacuums are more efficient on hard floors, but some models can handle low-pile carpets. It's important to check the specifications and reviews to ensure the vacuum meets your needs.

Do these vacuums work on all floor types?

While many budget robotic vacuums work well on hard floors, their performance on carpets and rugs can vary. It's advisable to choose a model with adjustable suction settings if you have a mix of floor types.
