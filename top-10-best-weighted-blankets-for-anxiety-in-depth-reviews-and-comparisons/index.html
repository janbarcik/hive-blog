  2. Material: Look for breathable materials like cotton or bamboo, especially if you tend to sleep hot.

FAQ Section

What is the best weighted blanket for anxiety?

The Gravity Blanket is widely regarded as the best weighted blanket for anxiety due to its superior design, comfort, and customer satisfaction.

What is the best weight for a weighted blanket for anxiety?

The ideal weight is typically 10% of your body weight. For example, a 150-pound individual should consider a 15-pound blanket.

Are weighted blankets safe for everyone?

Weighted blankets are generally safe for most adults and older children. However, they are not recommended for infants or very young children due to the risk of suffocation.

Can weighted blankets help with other conditions besides anxiety?

Yes, weighted blankets can also aid in improving sleep disorders, reducing stress, and providing comfort for individuals with ADHD or autism.

How do I care for my weighted blanket?

Most weighted blankets come with care instructions. Many have removable covers that are machine washable. Always follow the manufacturer's guidelines for the best results.
