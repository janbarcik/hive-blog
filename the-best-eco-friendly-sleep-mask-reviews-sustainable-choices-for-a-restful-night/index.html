FAQ Section

What makes a sleep mask eco-friendly?

Eco-friendly sleep masks are made from sustainable, biodegradable, or recycled materials. They often use organic fabrics like bamboo, hemp, or cotton and are produced with minimal environmental impact.

How do I choose the right eco-friendly sleep mask for sensitive skin?

Look for masks made from hypoallergenic materials such as organic bamboo or cotton. These materials are gentle on the skin and reduce the risk of irritation.

Are eco-friendly sleep masks more expensive than regular ones?

Eco-friendly sleep masks can be slightly more expensive due to the cost of sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing practices. However, they offer environmental benefits and often higher quality.

Can eco-friendly sleep masks block out all light?

While many eco-friendly sleep masks are effective at blocking light, some materials like thinner cotton may not block as much light as thicker materials like recycled polyester.

How should I care for my eco-friendly sleep mask?

Care instructions vary by material. Generally, masks made from cotton or bamboo can be machine washed, while silk masks require hand washing. Always check the manufacturer's care instructions.
