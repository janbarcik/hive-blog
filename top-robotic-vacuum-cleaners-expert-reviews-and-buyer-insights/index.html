FAQ Section

1. How often should I clean my robotic vacuum?

For optimal performance, clean the brushes and empty the dustbin after every use. Regularly check for tangled hair or debris.

2. Do robotic vacuum cleaners work on all floor types?

Yes, most robotic vacuums can handle a variety of surfaces, including hardwood, tile, and carpets. However, performance may vary depending on the model and the floor type.

3. Can robotic vacuums clean pet hair effectively?

Many robotic vacuums, like the iRobot Roomba i7+, are specifically designed to handle pet hair effectively. Look for models with strong suction and brushes designed for pet hair.

4. How long do robotic vacuum cleaners typically last?

With proper maintenance, a quality robotic vacuum cleaner can last anywhere from 3 to 5 years. Battery life may diminish over time, but replacements are often available.

5. Are robotic vacuum cleaners noisy?

Noise levels vary by model. Some, like the Eufy RoboVac 11S, are known for quiet operation, while others may be louder, especially on carpeted surfaces.
