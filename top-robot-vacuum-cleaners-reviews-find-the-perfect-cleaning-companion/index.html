FAQ Section

1. What should I consider when buying a robot vacuum cleaner?

When purchasing a robot vacuum cleaner, consider factors like suction power, battery life, navigation technology, smart features, and whether it includes a mopping function.

2. Are robot vacuum cleaners suitable for all floor types?

Most robot vacuum cleaners are designed to handle various floor types, including hardwood, carpet, and tile. However, models with mopping capabilities may be better suited for hard floors.

3. How often should I run my robot vacuum cleaner?

It depends on your household's needs. For homes with pets or high foot traffic, daily cleaning may be beneficial. Otherwise, running it a few times a week should suffice.

4. Do robot vacuum cleaners require maintenance?

Yes, regular maintenance is required. This includes emptying the dustbin, cleaning brushes and filters, and checking for any obstructions in the wheels or sensors.

5. Can robot vacuum cleaners replace traditional vacuums?

While robot vacuums are convenient for daily maintenance, they might not replace a traditional vacuum for deep cleaning tasks. They are best used as a complementary tool.
