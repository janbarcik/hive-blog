Buyer's Guide: 3 Key Factors to Consider

1. Suction Power

The primary function of a vacuum cleaner is to remove dust and debris. Even at a lower price point, prioritize models that offer sufficient suction power to suit your flooring type, whether it's hardwood, tile, or carpet.

2. Battery Life

Consider how long the vacuum can clean on a single charge. Models with longer battery life are more convenient as they require less frequent recharging.

3. Navigation Technology

Look for vacuums with intelligent navigation systems to ensure efficient cleaning paths and to avoid obstacles.
