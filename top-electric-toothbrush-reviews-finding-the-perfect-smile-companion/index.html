FAQ Section

1. What features should I look for in an electric toothbrush?

When choosing an electric toothbrush, consider features such as battery life, smart technology integration, variety of brush heads, ease of use, and additional modes for different cleaning needs.

2. How often should I replace the brush head on my electric toothbrush?

It is generally recommended to replace the brush head every three months, or sooner if the bristles become frayed.

3. Are electric toothbrushes better than manual ones?

Electric toothbrushes can be more effective at removing plaque and reducing gingivitis compared to manual toothbrushes, thanks to their advanced technology and consistent brushing action.

4. Can children use electric toothbrushes?

Yes, many electric toothbrushes are designed specifically for children, featuring smaller brush heads and gentle cleaning modes suitable for young users.

5. Do electric toothbrushes help with whitening teeth?

While electric toothbrushes primarily focus on cleaning and plaque removal, some models offer whitening modes and specialized brush heads that may help in reducing surface stains over time.
