FAQ Section

**1. What makes noise-cancelling headphones effective?**

Noise-cancelling headphones use active noise control technology to reduce unwanted ambient sounds. They create an opposite sound wave to cancel out external noises, providing a quieter listening experience.

**2. Are noise-cancelling headphones worth the investment?**

Yes, they are particularly beneficial for frequent travelers, commuters, and anyone working in noisy environments as they help improve focus and reduce distractions.

**3. How do I choose the right noise-cancelling headphones?**

Consider factors like noise cancellation quality, sound quality, comfort, battery life, and compatibility with your devices to find the right pair.

**4. Can noise-cancelling headphones damage your hearing?**

No, they do not damage your hearing. In fact, by reducing the need to increase volume in noisy environments, they can help protect your hearing over time.

**5. Do noise-cancelling headphones work without music?**

Yes, they can still reduce ambient noise even when you're not listening to music, making them useful for creating a quiet environment anywhere.
